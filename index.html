Purpose

This is meant to be used by Boulder Creek High School security and law enforcement if needed to better respond to lockdown situations and to keep order during ordinary school days.

Student Search
The Student Search enables security to search for a student and to see if they are checked out of their class.

School Map
Security can use the school map to select certain buildings and locations in that building to see how many students and the names of those students who have checked into the location during a lockdown or as their bathroom location.
